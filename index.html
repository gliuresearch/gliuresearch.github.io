Biography

I'm a Ph.D. student in the ECE department at Virginia Tech. My advisor is Dr. Haining Wang.

Before joining Virginia Tech with my advisor, I was a Ph.D. cadidate in the ECE department at the University of Delaware. I received my Bachelor's Degree at Purdue University, West Lafayette in 2016.
